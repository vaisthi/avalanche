🏔️ AVALANCHES

Nature's Powerful Snow Slides

❄️ What is an Avalanche?

An avalanche is the sudden movement of snow, ice, and rocks down a mountain slope. It happens when layers of snow become unstable and slide downhill because of gravity.

⚡ Causes of Avalanches

🌨️ Heavy Snowfall

Fresh snowfall increases pressure on older layers of snow.

🌡️ Rising Temperature

Warmer weather weakens the snow and causes it to slide.

🌍 Earthquakes

Ground vibrations may trigger an avalanche.

👣 Human Activity

Skiers and climbers can accidentally trigger avalanches.

⚠️ Effects of Avalanches

  • 🏠 Damage to homes and roads
  • 🚑 Loss of lives and injuries
  • 🌲 Destruction of forests
  • 🐻 Harm to wildlife

🛡️ Safety Measures

  • ✔ Check weather forecasts.
  • ✔ Carry emergency equipment.
  • ✔ Avoid dangerous slopes.
  • ✔ Follow avalanche warnings.
